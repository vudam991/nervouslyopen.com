Privacy & Data

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 4, 2026

Overview

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our apps are designed with privacy in mind—neither SleepySheepie nor Journly require account creation or sign-in, and neither app intentionally collects personal information from users.

Information We Collect

In-App Data

  • SleepySheepie: Does not collect personal data. The app focuses on providing a calm bedtime experience.
  • Journly: Your journal entries are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to your journal entries.

Support Communications

If you contact support by email, we may receive:

  • Your email address
  • Any information you choose to include in your message
  • Details about your device and app version (if you provide them)

We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and provide support.

Analytics

We do not currently use analytics or tracking services in our apps.

How We Use Information

We use information you provide only to:

  • Respond to your support inquiries
  • Troubleshoot app issues
  • Improve our apps based on your feedback

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose beyond providing support.

Sharing and Storage

We do not intentionally share personal information with third parties. Information you send by email is:

  • Stored in our email provider
  • Handled only as needed for support purposes
  • Not shared with external companies or services

Data Retention

Support email correspondence is retained only as long as necessary to resolve your issue and provide ongoing support. You may request deletion of your email records at any time.

Your Choices

Access, Update, or Delete

  • SleepySheepie: No personal data is collected, so there is no personal information to access or delete.
  • Journly: Your journal entries are stored locally on your device and are under your complete control. You can delete entries or the entire app at any time.

Support Correspondence

If you have contacted support and want us to delete your email correspondence, please let us know by replying to the support email thread.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information you provide. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with information, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact:

Email: iam@no-benefit.com

We will respond to privacy inquiries as soon as possible.
