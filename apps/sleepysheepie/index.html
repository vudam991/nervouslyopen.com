About SleepySheepie

SleepySheepie is designed to help you relax at night, reduce friction in your bedtime routine, and access sleep-focused tools in one place. The app requires no account or sign-in—just open it and start your bedtime routine.

Key Features

  • No account required
  • Calm, bedtime-focused experience
  • Simple, intuitive design
  • Supports a smoother nighttime routine
  • Lightweight and fast
  • Works completely offline
