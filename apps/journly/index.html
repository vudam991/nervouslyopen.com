About Journly

Journly is designed to be your personal space for reflection and self-discovery. Whether you journal daily or occasionally, Journly provides a simple, private place to capture your thoughts, ideas, and memories.

Key Features

  • Distraction-free writing interface
  • All entries stored locally on your device
  • No account or sign-in required
  • Privacy-first approach
  • Easy date navigation
  • Search your entries
  • Customizable themes
  • Works completely offline
